The Berkeley Difference

Here's What Sets Berkeley Apart

Straight from their own program pages — these are the differentiators Berkeley College consistently emphasizes, and what employers and graduates confirm.

Industry-Developed Curriculum

Programs are developed with input from Advisory Boards made up of industry experts — ensuring you learn skills employers actually demand, not just academic theory.

Real-World Faculty Experience

Professors are chosen for academic excellence and relevant professional experience. You learn from people who've worked in your field, not just studied it.

Stackable Credentials

Start with a Certificate, earn your Associate's, then Bachelor's — all at Berkeley, with credits transferring seamlessly. The 4-year combined BBA/MBA lets you earn both degrees in the time most schools give you one.

Campus, Online, or Both

Attend at Berkeley's NYC Midtown, Newark, Woodbridge, or Woodland Park campuses — or study fully online through Berkeley College Online®, ranked by U.S. News 13 years running.

Career Services & Internships

Berkeley's Career Services team connects students with internships and employers before graduation. NYC campus students have unparalleled access to business, finance, fashion, and media industries.

Veterans & Military Benefits

Berkeley is a Yellow Ribbon program participant and accepts GI Bill® benefits. Many programs are available for active duty, veterans, and eligible dependents — online and on campus.
