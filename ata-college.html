What You'll Train For

Healthcare Programs at ATA College

Every ATA program is built around real-world, hands-on training. You won't just study healthcare — you'll practice it in clinical settings with instructors who've worked in the field.

🩺
Practical Nursing (LPN)

One of ATA's most popular programs. Graduate prepared to sit for the NCLEX-PN and step into LPN roles in hospitals, clinics, and long-term care. Kentucky Board of Nursing — Full Approval.

KY Board Approved NCLEX-PN Prep Clinical Hours
🏥
Medical Assisting

One of the most versatile roles in healthcare. Medical Assistants handle both clinical and administrative duties — essential in virtually every type of medical practice.

Diploma & AOS Options Externship Included
🦷
Dental Assisting

Work alongside dentists in one of the most stable healthcare sectors. Graduates have placed at private practices and specialty clinics across ATA's markets.

Hands-On Lab Career Placement
💻
Medical Coding

Translate diagnoses and procedures into standardized codes used for insurance reimbursement — a growing field with strong remote work potential and clear certification pathways.

Remote-Friendly CPC Exam Prep
🔬
Diagnostic Medical Sonography

Use ultrasound technology to help diagnose patients. DMS is one of the highest-paying ATA programs and one of the highest-demand imaging specialties in the country ($31,850 program cost).

High-Demand Specialty ARDMS Prep
❤️
Echocardiography Technology

Specialize in cardiac imaging. Echo techs work in hospitals and cardiology practices — a highly specialized and well-compensated healthcare career path.

Cardiac Specialty Strong Salary Outlook
🔪
Surgical Technician

Work as part of a lifesaving surgical team in hospitals, medical centers, and surgical centers. ATA's Associate of Applied Science program prepares you for the NBSTSA certification exam — completable in as fast as 24 months.

AAS Degree NBSTSA Exam Prep 24 Months
📋
Healthcare Management & Leadership

For healthcare workers ready to move into management. This Bachelor of Science completer program is offered fully online — designed for working professionals looking to advance.

Bachelor's Degree 100% Online
📡
Limited Medical Radiography

Learn to perform diagnostic X-ray procedures. Radiography technologists are needed in urgent care centers, outpatient clinics, and physicians' offices across every market.

Imaging Technology Blended Format
